What Is

Hypnosis, also known as hypnotherapy or hypnotic suggestion, is a state similar to trance in which you increase focus and concentration. Hypnosis is usually done with the help of a therapist using verbal repetition and mental images.
When you are under hypnosis, usually feels calm and relaxed, and is more open to suggestions. Hypnosis can be used to help you gain control over unwanted behaviors or to help you cope better with anxiety or pain. It is important to know that although you are more open to suggestions during hypnosis, you don't lose control over your behavior.

WHAT IS POSSIBLE TO TREAT WITH HYPNOSIS?

Hypnotherapy is an effective method to deal with stress and anxiety. In particular, hypnosis can reduce stress and anxiety, acting actively in the treatment of chemical dependence, addictions, bad habits, traumas and phobias. It also helps to unlock limitations and insecurities, all without any risk.

IS IT SAFE? DO I LOSE CONTROL OVER MYSELF?

It's safe, the trance state is not the same as sleep profound. In trance, the person becomes extremely focused on something specific, but he's not sleeping. In the trance state, the patient is able to conduct his thoughts and can remember everything he has heard and said.
